The Devotion of Savitri

The tale of Savitri and Satyavan is a poignant love story from the Mahabharata that symbolizes the power of love and devotion. Savitri, a devoted wife, marries Satyavan, who is destined to die one year after their marriage.

When Yama, the god of death, comes to take Satyavan, Savitri follows him, arguing for her husband’s life. Impressed by her determination and love, Yama grants her a boon. She cleverly asks for the return of Satyavan’s life.

This tale emphasizes the themes of fidelity, devotion, and the triumph of love over death.
