The Life of Krishna

Krishna, one of the most revered deities in Hinduism, is celebrated as the embodiment of love, compassion, and divine playfulness. Born in a prison cell to Devaki and Vasudeva, he was destined to defeat the evil king Kansa.

His childhood is filled with enchanting tales of mischief, bravery, and divine miracles, including lifting a mountain to protect his devotees. Krishna is also known for his teachings in the Bhagavad Gita, where he imparts spiritual wisdom to the warrior Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.

His life stories inspire devotion, illustrating the ideals of love, friendship, and moral righteousness.
