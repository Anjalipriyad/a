A Glimpse into the Ramayana

The Ramayana is one of the two great epics of ancient India, written by the sage Valmiki. It tells the story of Prince Rama, his wife Sita, and his loyal companion Hanuman as they embark on a quest to rescue Sita from the demon king Ravana.

Rama, the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu, is depicted as the ideal hero, embodying dharma (righteousness). His journey takes him through various trials, including the abduction of Sita, battles with Ravana, and the ultimate triumph of good over evil.

The epic emphasizes the importance of duty, honor, and love, showcasing the struggles of its characters and their unwavering commitment to righteousness.
