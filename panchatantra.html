Wisdom in Panchatantra Tales

The Panchatantra is a collection of ancient Indian fables, attributed to Vishnu Sharma. It consists of stories featuring animals that teach moral lessons and practical wisdom.

Each tale conveys a unique lesson, often revolving around themes of friendship, cunning, and the consequences of greed. One popular story is that of the "Tortoise and the Geese," which teaches the importance of discretion and caution in communication.

The Panchatantra has influenced storytelling across cultures and remains a cherished part of Indian folklore.
